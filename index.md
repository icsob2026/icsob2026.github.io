<p class="font-weight-normal">The International Conference on Software Business (ICSOB) 2026 brings together researchers and practitioners who are concerned with software-intensive business in different ways. The conference serves as a platform for sharing findings and experiences from the field of software-intensive business, addressing the management, development, and evolution of software-intensive products and services. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, software product management, software ecosystems, open source software business, agile and lean methods, AI-driven software development, and digital transformation. ICSOB 2026 invites interdisciplinary collaboration among researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to advance knowledge and practice in software business.</p>
